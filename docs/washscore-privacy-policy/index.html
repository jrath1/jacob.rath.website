WashScore Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

WashScore respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the WashScore mobile application.

Information We Collect

WashScore may collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Vehicle information you enter
  • Wash history and notes
  • Product and garage information
  • App preferences and settings

WashScore does not require users to create an account.

WashScore may also access:

  • Your device location, with permission, to provide local weather forecasts and wash recommendations
  • Notification permissions, with permission, to send wash day reminders and forecast alerts

How Information Is Used

WashScore uses information solely to:

  • Generate weather-based wash recommendations
  • Provide forecast and wash score data
  • Send optional notifications and reminders
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

Your garage information, wash history, notes, and preferences are stored locally on your device unless future cloud-sync features are added.

WashScore does not currently sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Weather Data

WashScore uses weather data provided by Open-Meteo to provide forecasts and wash recommendations.

Notifications

If enabled, WashScore may send notifications related to:

  • Recommended wash days
  • Forecast updates
  • Reminder alerts

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Location Permissions

Location access is optional but recommended for accurate local weather forecasts and wash recommendations.

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Data Deletion

Because WashScore stores data locally on your device, you may delete your data by removing information within the app or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

WashScore is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
