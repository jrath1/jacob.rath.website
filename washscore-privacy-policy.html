WashScore Privacy Policy

WashScore helps users make better laundry-day decisions with weather-aware reminders and recommendations.

Information We Collect

WashScore may collect information you provide directly, such as preferences and support requests, along with device and usage information needed to operate the app.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide, maintain, and improve WashScore, personalize recommendations, and respond to support requests.

Data Storage

Data is stored only as needed to provide the service. We do not sell personal information.

Weather Data

Weather information is retrieved from third-party weather services to provide forecasts and laundry recommendations.

Notifications

You may enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Location Permissions

Location access is used to provide local weather information. You can change this permission in your device settings.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact jrath1@comcast.net.

Children's Privacy

WashScore is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves.

Contact

Questions can be sent to jrath1@comcast.net.
